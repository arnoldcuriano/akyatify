The Problem

Filipino hikers deserve better tools.

Right now, the community runs on Facebook groups, chat threads, and word of mouth. That is not good enough when you are planning climbs, tracking progress, and staying safe on the trail.

No local trail maps

Generic apps do not know Apo, Pulag, or Batulao. We are building maps for Philippine peaks with elevation, difficulty, and offline access.

No summit tracking

There is nowhere to log your conquered peaks, build a climbing record, or show your community what you have achieved.

Guides have no platform

Local guides run their business through scattered posts and messages. They deserve a professional booking system that builds credibility.

Safety is an afterthought

Most apps do not offer live GPS sharing or SOS features. On remote mountains, that should never be optional.

What We're Building

Built around how Filipinos actually hike.

Not a generic trail app with Philippine peaks added later. A platform designed from the ground up for this community.

Free

Trail Map and Navigation

Philippine-specific trail maps with elevation profiles, difficulty ratings, and crowd-sourced trail conditions. Basic Google Maps integration from day one.

Free

Community Feed

Share trip photos, post trail condition updates, and plan group hikes. A social layer built for hikers, not a generic network.

Peak Log

Mountain Conquest Log

Track every peak you have summited. Build your climbing history with photos, dates, and elevation stats, then share your summit card.

Pro

Offline Maps and Live GPS

Download trail maps before you hike, share your live location with trusted contacts, and use one-tap SOS with emergency alerts.

Free

Guide Marketplace

Browse verified local tour guides by trail, price, and reviews. Guides get a professional profile, booking calendar, and GCash payout flow.

Pro

Smart Recommendations

Trail suggestions based on your skill level, conquered peaks, and season so you always know what mountain to climb next.
